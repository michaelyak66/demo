Who We Are

NITORTS NIG LTD is a leading soft hospital facility management company based in Lagos, Nigeria. Our mission is to provide comprehensive, high-quality facility management services that ensure the health, safety, and well-being of patients, staff, and visitors in healthcare environments. We adhere strictly to Joint Commission International (JCI) standards, ensuring that our services meet the highest global benchmarks for healthcare quality and safety.

Our Vision

To be the foremost provider of hospital facility management services in Nigeria, renowned for excellence, reliability, and adherence to international standards.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional facility management services that support healthcare institutions in providing safe, clean, and efficient environments conducive to healing and wellness.
