Who We Are

NITORTS is a leading soft hospital facility management company based in Lagos, Nigeria. Our mission is to provide comprehensive, high-quality facility management services that ensure the health, safety, and well-being of patients, staff, and visitors in healthcare environments. We adhere strictly to Joint Commission International (JCI) standards, ensuring that our services meet the highest global benchmarks for healthcare quality and safety.

Our Vision

To be the foremost provider of hospital facility management services in Nigeria, renowned for excellence, reliability, and adherence to international standards.

Our Mission

To deliver exceptional facility management services that support healthcare institutions in providing safe, clean, and efficient environments conducive to healing and wellness.

Services

  • Routine and deep cleaning of patient rooms, operating theaters, and common areas.
  • Infection control cleaning to prevent the spread of healthcare-associated infections (HAIs).
  • Waste management, including hazardous and medical waste disposal.

  • Regular inspections and treatments to prevent and control pest infestations.
  • Use of non-toxic and eco-friendly pest control methods to ensure patient safety.

  • Proper handling and laundering of hospital linens and uniforms.
  • Ensuring strict adherence to hygiene standards to prevent contamination.

  • Providing trained security personnel to ensure the safety of patients, staff, and visitors.
  • Implementing access control systems and surveillance to monitor sensitive areas.

  • Managing front desk operations and assisting patients and visitors with inquiries.
  • Providing information and support to enhance patient and visitor experience.

  • Efficient and safe disposal of general, medical, and hazardous waste.
  • Compliance with environmental and health regulations for waste management.

  • Safe and efficient transport of patients within the healthcare facility.
  • Ensuring timely and comfortable transfers to different departments or locations.

  • Regular upkeep of administrative offices, waiting areas, and staff lounges.
  • Ensuring these areas are clean, functional, and comfortable.

  • Providing ongoing training to staff on JCI standards and best practices.
  • Ensuring all personnel are knowledgeable about infection control, safety protocols, and customer service.

  • Monitoring and managing air and water quality within the facility.
  • Implementing energy-efficient practices and sustainability initiatives.

  • Developing and managing recycling programs to reduce waste.
  • Promoting environmental sustainability through proper waste segregation and disposal.

  • Establishing systems for receiving and addressing patient and visitor feedback.
  • Implementing continuous improvement processes based on feedback.
